Our Products

Wheel Chair Pad

Wheelchair cushions are cushions specifically designed to provide comfort and protection against injury for wheelchair users. They also aid in properly positioning the user in the correct posture.

Mollify Diabetic Insole

Diabetic shoes and inserts are prescribed to patients with a diagnosis of diabetes who will benefit from orthotic intervention to prevent development of wounds on the feet. Many shoe styles are available for careful selection based on evaluation of your feet, ankles, and goals for daily use.

Maximal Toe Spreader

Toe spreaders are contraptions that fit between the toes and hold them spread apart. You instantly get better circulation, and as you walk around in them, your toe bones and muscles gradually restored to better alignment.

Megnetic Arc Suppport

The arch of the foot functions to absorb shock, store it, and then convert that energy into propulsion during the gait cycle, Arch support is important for keeping your feet and body aligned to help prevent injury

Mollify Rocker Insole

An addition or modification made to the bottom of the shoe designed to address ROM limitations, offload an area on the bottom of the foot or improve gait patterns.
